Pick a Pres

In this game, you'll have the opportunity to pick our next President!

Rules:

First, you'll select a candidate, then answer a few questions about the candidates.

  • Get the question right, and you'll score one letter in your candidate's name.
  • Get an answer wrong, and the other candidate will get a letter.
  • First candidate to have all the letters in their name will be your next President!
